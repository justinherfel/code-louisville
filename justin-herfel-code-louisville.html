Introduction

I have traveled and lived in many different places, and have chronicled some of the areas I lived over the years. This website features a small sample of pictures taken in 2020, when I packed all my worldly possessions into my Toyota Camry and left Los Angeles. The 2,100 journey took me through some spectacular scenery. Here are some samples of what I saw. Enjoy!
