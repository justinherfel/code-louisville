Introduction

I have traveled and lived in many different places, and have chronicled some of the areas I lived over the years. This website features a small sample of pictures taken in 2020, when I packed all my worldly possessions into my Toyota Camry and left Los Angeles. The 2,100 mile journey took me through California, Arizona, New Mexico, Texas, Oklahoma, Kansas, Illinois, Indiana and Kentucky. The most stressful part of the journey was on the first night, where I could not find a hotel in Arizona, so I had to sleep in my car at a rest stop. I visited White Sands National Park twice. My favorite state was New Mexico. The desert there is spectacular, the people are friendly and things are quite inexpensive. Here are some samples of what I saw. Enjoy!
